I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Sciences and Engineering at the OSU. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Ness Shroff. In Spring and Summer 2022, I spent a wonderful time as a visiting student in AINet-Lab at ZJU, supervised by Prof. Yang Yang. Before joining the OSU, I graduated from the Department of Mechanical Engineering at SJTU.

My research interests include machine learning, graph mining, computer vision, robotics and neural computing. Currently, I'm particularly interested in the graph condensation technologies that ease the graph representation learning (e.g., GNN deployment) in real industrial scenarios. I also pay special attention to incorporating reinforcement learning into the graph mining tasks.
